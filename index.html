Wasim Harwill Info

Artistic practice in film and theater: as an actor, director, and playwright. The trajectory, from being outside the cultural world and unaware of its existence, to engaging and involving it from this position. Creating art that criticizes and challenges the way people see the world, comments on our contemporary times, hopefully with creations that lead to self-reflection and possibly even change.
